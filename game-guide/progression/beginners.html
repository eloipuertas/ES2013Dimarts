    Beginners Guide
    Welcome to HAWKEN.

    Meet Laila. When you launch HAWKEN as a new pilot, she’ll walk you through your first few levels and introduce you to the concepts you’ll need to master if you want to survive long enough to become a seasoned veteran.

    Piloting Your First Mech
    Complete VR Training with Laila

    You will begin your training in a controlled, virtual reality environment. Click TRAINING and select VR TRAINING. Laila will upload you to the VR training grounds and show you how to control your mech.

    Introducing Fred, your brand new CR-T Recruit mech. Learn the basics of piloting a mech in VR training.

    Stepping Out Of VR Training
    Practice Against Training Bots in Offline Team Deathmatch

    After your VR Training, you can group up with a team of bots in Offline Team Deathmatch. You won't gain XP or HC, but it's a good way to practice before playing against live opponents.

    Practice firing at fast-moving targets. Learn to lead enemies with missiles and side-dash out of the path of enemy projectiles. Get a feel for how much heat your weapons generate and how much fuel your thrusters consume.

    HUD Legend

    Your First Live Match
    Prove Yourself in Team Deathmatch to Unlock Additional Game Modes

    Once you’ve destroyed a few bots in training, it’s time to play against other players. At first, you can only play Team Deathmatch. It’s you and your teammates fighting against another team.

    After three rounds of Team Deathmatch you will begin to unlock additional game modes.

    Click PLAY, then QUICK MATCH, and LAUNCH to start your first live match.

    You will enter the staging area before a match and between respawns.
    STAGING: Choose Your Mech, Primary Weapon, Countermeasures, and Holo Emotes

    After you click READY in the Lobby, you’ll enter the Staging Area. Here you can choose which mech you want to play, activate or swap your countermeasures, and change your primary weapon. Click LAUNCH in the upper left to join the battle.

    Find your teammates on the radar, group up with them, and start hunting down enemies. Be ready, these are live opponents, not bots, so they’ll be less predictable and battles tend to get chaotic.

    The Mech Garage
    ITEMS AND INTERNALS: Purchase Equipment in the Garage to Expand Your Options in Battle

    After your first few matches, spend some time in the garage to check out all the equipment you can add to your mech.

    Items are deployable equipment such as the Hologram and Shield. Internals are passive enhancements such as the Deflector and Repair Kit.

    To see the equipment available for purchase, choose a mech in your garage and click ITEMS or INTERNALS.

    Check out the Items Guide for more information on items.

    TUNING: Use Modification Points to Improve your Mech’s Performance

    As you earn pilot levels, you will gain extra modification points. Every point you use provides an incremental performance improvement to that system.

    To modify your mech’s systems, select a mech from your garage and click TUNING.

    For more information on allocating modification points, check out the Tuning Guide.

    STYLE: Customize the Appearance of Your Mech

    HAWKEN gives you plenty of options to stand out on the battlefield. Select a mech and click STYLE to access cosmetic customizations, such as:

    • Mech body parts and custom chassis
    • Thrusters and repair drones
    • Camouflage, paint, and trim
    • Mech emotes and holographic emotes
    Most style options require Meteor Credits (MC) to purchase. If you need more MC, you can purchase them here or click BUY METEOR CREDITS at the top of the game menu.
    Pilot Progression
    Gain Pilot Levels to Unlock Free Mechs, New Equipment, and Modification Points

    As you earn experience and reach higher pilot levels, you’ll unlock more and more options for playing, customizing, upgrading, and tuning your mechs.

    Every time you gain a pilot level, you may earn one or more of these rewards:

    • New drones, thrusters, and camos
    • Access to new item and internal groups
    • Extra modification points to tune your mechs’ systems
    • Access to a free new mech!
    Mech Progression
    Upgrade your Mechs to Unlock Powerful Rewards

    Once your mech has earned enough XP, you can upgrade it to the next mech rank. Select the mech you want to upgrade and click MECH RANK to start. Rank upgrades are purchased with HAWKEN Credits (HC) or Meteor Credits (MC) and unlock a variety of rewards:

    • Alternate weapons
    • Special ability upgrades
    • Elite mech body parts
    • Elite camo
    • Universal item unlock (available on all of your mechs)
    • Access to a new free mech!

    For more information on upgrading your mechs, check out the Upgrading Mechs Guide.

    XP TRANSFER: Transfer Overflow XP to Other Mechs
    When you upgrade a mech to rank 5 and reach MAX XP, any additional XP you earn with that mech can be converted into Universal XP.

    Universal XP can be transferred to any other mech you own to help you upgrade those mechs faster.

    The XP Transfer menu can by accessed two ways:

    • Click the XP icon at the top of the screen.
    • Enter the garage and open the MECH RANK menu.

    For more information on mech progression, check out the Upgrading Mechs Guide.
