    Game Guide / Progression / F.A.Q.
    Frequently Asked Questions

    Below are the answers to some of the most frequently asked questions about the HAWKEN progression system.

    Have a question that’s not answered here? Check out the HAWKEN Support page.

    Mech Progression
    • How do I gain mech XP? Your mechs gain XP as you play HAWKEN. You gain XP for many actions during matches, such as destroying and assisting in the destruction of enemy mechs, capturing and controlling objectives, and running energy. XP is awarded at the end of each match.
    • How can I gain mech XP faster? You can purchase an XP Boost to double all the XP you gain while the boost is active. You purchase XP Boosts in the game client with Meteor Credits. The XP Boost becomes active at the beginning of your next match after purchase.
    • Why isn't my mech XP going up? When your mech gains enough XP to upgrade to a new rank, all XP gained goes into the Overflow XP pool until you upgrade that mech. You can pay for mech upgrades with either HAWKEN Credits (HC) or Meteor Credits (MC).
    • What do I get for unlocking new mech ranks? Every new mech rank rewards pilots with more optimization choices and elite mech parts you can use to customize the look of your mech. Once your mech reaches max XP, you are rewarded with a universal two-slot item you can equip on any mech, the Veteran Camo for that mech, and a brand new mech.
    • What happens to my mech’s experience when it reaches max XP? When a mech has been upgraded to max rank and reaches max XP, all experience earned then goes into the XP Overflow pool. You can spend MC to transfer Overflow XP into the Global XP pool. Global XP can be freely added to other mechs.
    • How do I unlock new mechs? New pilots can unlock every mech in HAWKEN just by playing the game. You can earn five free mechs (plus your starting mech) by earning pilot levels. You can unlock all other mechs for free by reaching max XP on the mechs you own. You can also purchase new mechs using HAWKEN Credits (HC) or Meteor Credits (MC). See our Unlocking Mechs guide for more details.
    Pilot Progression
    • How do I gain pilot XP? You gain pilot experience as you play HAWKEN matches. All the XP your mechs gain during a match is also applied to your pilot level.
    • How can I gain pilot XP faster? You can purchase an XP Boost to double all the experience you gain while the boost is active. You purchase XP Boosts in the game client with Meteor Credits. The XP Boost becomes active at the beginning of your next match after purchase.
    • What do I get for gaining pilot levels? Earning new pilot levels unlocks numerous rewards, such as access to more powerful items and internals, exclusive thrusters and repair drones, and even free mechs.
    Items, Internals, and Weapons
    • How do I unlock items/internals? You unlock items and internals through pilot progression. As you gain pilot levels, you unlock more and larger items and internals. You can purchase unlocked equipment with either HAWKEN Credits (HC) or Meteor Credits (MC). For more about unlocking and purchasing items and internals, see the Upgrading Mechs guide.
    • Why can’t I equip the item/internal I purchased on my other mechs? Items and internals are purchased for a single mech only. You can unlock special, universal items, which can be equipped on any mech by getting a mech to max XP.
    • Can I change items/internals during a match? No. You can only equip items and internals in the garage between matches.
    • How do I unlock other weapons on my mech? The alternate primary weapon is unlocked at mech rank 1, and you can purchase it with either HAWKEN Credits (HC) or Meteor Credits (MC). A mech’s prestige weapons unlocks once you upgrade that mech to rank 3, and you can then purchase it with HC or MC. To purchase a new weapon, select a mech and click WEAPONS. See the Upgrading Mechs guide for more information.
    • Can I change primary weapons during a match? Yes. You can change weapons in the Staging area during a match by clicking your primary weapon and selecting another weapon you have purchased.
    Mech Tuning
    • Why are some of my tuning slots locked? All mech systems have four locked slots at rank 1. You unlock one additional column of slots every time you upgrade your mech to a higher rank. To unlock all of the tuning slot columns, you must upgrade your mech to rank 5. See the Tuning guide for more information.
    • Why don’t I have any modification points? You don’t have any modification points until you reach pilot level 5. At that point, you earn 1 mod point every level until level 30, when you earn an additional 5 points. Thus, a level 30 pilot will have 30 modification points.
    • Can I use my modification points on all my mechs? Yes. Your total modification points can be used on every mech you own. You do not need to divide them up between mechs. Max level pilots can allocate 30 mod points to each and every mech they own.
    • Can I change how my mech is tuned during a match? No. You can only allocate modification points to your mech’s systems in the garage between matches.
    Currency
    • How do I get HAWKEN Credits (HC)? You earn HAWKEN Credits (HC) by playing the game. At the end of every match, you earn an amount of HC based on the time you spent in the match, along with bonuses for winning and placing highly.
    • How can I get HAWKEN Credits faster? You can purchase an HC Boost to double all the HAWKEN Credits you earn while the boost is active. You purchase HC Boosts in the game client with Meteor Credits. The HC Boost becomes active at the beginning of your next match after purchase.
    • How do I purchase Meteor Credits (MC)? You can purchase Meteor Credits (MC) by visiting our Currency page. Your MC will be credited to your account as soon as you complete your purchase. You can also access the MC currency page from inside the game client.
