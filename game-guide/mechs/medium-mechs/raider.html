    Raider
    Very Short-Range Burst Damage
    The Raider is designed to close gaps quickly and unleash a devastating barrage of firepower at close range. Its signature Blitz special ability increases movement speed and allows both weapons to be fired while boosting forward.
    sentium 03.05.2013 | Medium Type: OFFENSE Family: ASSASSINATION
    Mech Mechanics: raider
    Highlights: raider
    Special Ability: Blitz
    Increases your mech's movement speed for a short duration. This unique Special Ability also allows you to fire weapons while using your thrusters to boost forward.
    Gameplay Tip
    Use the Blitz special ability to quickly charge at unsuspecting enemies and catch them off guard before they can react.
    • Rank 1
      +1.6 m/s Run Speed While Active
    • Rank 2
      +2.5 m/s Boost Speed While Active
    • Rank 3
      -15% Cooldown
    • Rank 4
      +9% Duration
    • Rank 5
      +12% Accuracy While Boosting

    Unlocking the Raider

    To unlock the Raider for free, achieve MAX Rank on the Infiltrator.

    To learn about unlocking mechs, check out the Unlocking Mechs Guide.

    Piloting the Raider

    Activate Blitz and quickly traverse the map to claim objectives and assist allies.

    Use Blitz without boosting to approach enemies quickly without showing up on their radar.

    Defeating the Raider

    The Raider is incredibly dangerous at close range, so attack from a distance and be prepared for it to close the gap with Blitz.

    The Raider can also use the Corsair-KLA to fire long-range projectiles, so stay alert even at a distance.
