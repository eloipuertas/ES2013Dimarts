    Primary Weapons

    Every mech starts with one primary weapon. You can purchase two additional primary weapons in the garage. You can purchase the alternate primary weapon at Mech Rank 1 and the prestige weapon at Mech Rank 3.

    You can swap your primary weapon in the Garage or in the Staging Area before a match or between respawns.

    To learn more about unlocking new primary weapons, view the Upgrading Mechs Guide.
